# GenAIOps: Centre for Generative AI Operations

**Type:** Non-profit community initiative
**Focus:** Advancing Generative AI Operations

## Overview

GenAIOps is a community-driven initiative dedicated to making generative AI trustworthy, effective, and accountable. The organization transforms practical experience into shared knowledge, providing frameworks and guidance for organizations adopting generative AI.

## Mission

**"Take what we learn, shape it into collective wisdom, and give it back to the community."**

Goals:
- Build responsible future for generative AI
- Share real-world operational insights
- Create actionable frameworks
- Unite practitioners globally

## The GenAIOps Framework

### Three Pillars

The comprehensive model addresses:

| Pillar | Question | Component |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Why | Why organizations act | Generative AI Manifesto |
| How | How they deliver | GenAIOps Operating Model |
| What | What technologies to use | Generative AI Stack |

### 1. Generative AI Manifesto

**Guiding principles for AI adoption.**

Establishes the "why" behind organizational AI initiatives:
- Values and ethics
- Accountability frameworks
- Responsible development
- Trust building

### 2. GenAIOps Operating Model

**Exploration to production pathway.**

Addresses the "how" of delivery:
- Experimentation phases
- Validation processes
- Production deployment
- Operational management

### 3. Generative AI Stack

**Technical architecture layers.**

Defines the "what" of implementation:
- Infrastructure components
- Model layer
- Application layer
- Integration patterns

## The Bottom Line

GenAIOps fills the gap between traditional MLOps and the unique operational challenges of generative AI. By uniting practitioners globally and transforming real-world experience into shared frameworks, the community provides practical guidance for organizations moving from experimentation to production. The three-pillar approach—manifesto, operating model, and technical stack—offers a comprehensive framework for responsible AI adoption. As a non-profit initiative, it prioritizes collective wisdom over commercial interests.
