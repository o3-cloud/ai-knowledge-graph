# 2026: The Year The IDE Died

**Speakers:** Steve Yegge (Engineering Leader, Sourcegraph/Amp) & Gene Kim (Author & Researcher, IT Revolution)
**Uploader:** AI Engineer
**Upload Date:** December 6, 2025

## Overview

Steve Yegge and Gene Kim explore the significant lag between AI capability advances and developer adoption, investigating why software engineers globally remain 9-12 months behind the technology curve. They share predictions for 2026's AI coding tools and paint a vision of the future of development.

## Key Themes

### Developer-AI Technology Gap

Despite AI capabilities advancing rapidly, developers have consistently resisted adopting the most powerful tools available. This creates a significant lag where developers operate with outdated workflows while more capable solutions exist.

### IDE Transformation in 2026

The speakers predict that traditional IDEs will undergo radical transformation, moving from static development environments to AI-augmented, agent-capable systems. The "IDE as we know it" will fundamentally change as AI agents become first-class development partners.
